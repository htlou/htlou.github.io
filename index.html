Hi, I'm Hantao Lou!

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student ('26) at Yuanpei College, Peking University.

Currently I'm doing research at Center of AI Safety and Governance, Institute of Aritificial Intelligence, Peking University. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yaodong Yang. Also I was a scholar at MATS program supervised by Evan Hubinger.

At Peking University, I'm a member and the monitor of Tong Class, an pilot class in Aritificial Intelligence.

Most of my current research is driven by trying to find an answer to the question of:

How to make advanced, complex AI systems reliable and scalable?

To answer this question, I'm actively exploring the intersection of reinforcement learning, mechanistic interpretability, and their application in AI alignment, including but not limited to scalable oversight and formal verification.
